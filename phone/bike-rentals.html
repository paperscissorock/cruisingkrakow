Guys Beach Cruiser

Trekking Bike

Trekking Bike

 

If you're thinking of taking a longer trip but still want to remain comfortable then our trekking bikes are what you need. With 28 inch wheels these bike will get you there fast.  Krakow has numerous day or multi-day options, such as the Salt Mines in Wieliczka, the royal summer palace in Niepołomice, or the birth place of Pope John Paul II in Wadowice.  These bikes are great for the long haul. With an adjustable handle bar pivot we can change the angle to give you more comfort yet still remain in a sporty position. Contact us for ideas about possible trips. We can help find a route that suits you best.
